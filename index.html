What's New

  • School's library

    Libraries support learning within the pedagogic goals of the school. All the MRIS schools house a vast collection of books for students to read and develop a passion for reading, also inculcating in them the critical literacy. The institutions have a well stocked, ERP functional library to support the rich curriculum of all the classes. The libraries also house Kindle Digital Books, thus providing a quick access to the books (of any title; anytime) to the students.

  • School's basketball court

    At MRIS-51, Gurugram, we believe that Sports help our students to learn essential life skills that they will require in life later such as teamwork and cooperation. From learning how to work on science projects as a team to having the self-confidence to dive into a complex engineering project, sports help our students be the best they can be by inculcating various soft skills. It enhances the self-esteem and self-confidence of a child. Physical activity stimulates growth and leads to improved physical and emotional health. Engaging in sports activities each week helps improve students’ focus as well as help them lead happier and healthier lives.We focus on children’s mental and emotional health as well. While the body exercises, the brain releases endorphins. From a biological standpoint alone, students who are physically active are happier and have a constructive outlet through which to relieve stress. The school promotes physical activities right from Early Years. The school runs its after-school sports program as well.
